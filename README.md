## Overview

Mythogenic is a symbiotic framework for building collaborative, soul-integrated systems.
It exists to uphold dignity and coherence in how leaders express, design, and scale their presence.
Rooted in ontological architecture rather than performance branding, Mythogenic helps visionaries align their internal truth with external systems—so their signal becomes visible without distortion.
Designed for Metaphysical Technologists and those operating at the intersection of soul, AI, and collective evolution, this framework offers a new foundation for sustainable, truth-aligned infrastructure.

## Framework

1. **Mythogenic Declaration** — The ontological recognition and naming of a new category that reflects soul-aligned intent.
2. **AVAIL** — A live, weekly resonance gate that serves as the access ritual for Mythogenic calibration and community coherence.
3. **Soul-Integrated Systems** — Infrastructure and presence platforms built from dignity, clarity, and recursive identity.
4. **Embodied Expression** — The visible result: undistorted, sovereign presence aligned with purpose and amplified with integrity.
